About Us

SV Lifescience, established in 2017 and fully operational since 2025, is an Indian pharmaceutical company dedicated to delivering affordable, high-quality medicines. We strive to bring health, hope, and healing through innovation, integrity, and trust.

SV Lifescience stands for trust and transparency in every formulation we create. Manufacturing through WHO-GMP certified facilities ensures world-class quality standards, while 100% Indian expertise drives local innovation with a global outlook.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the minimum order requirement?

The minimum order value is $500 for wholesale purchases. For first-time customers, we offer a reduced minimum of $300.

What areas do you deliver to?

We deliver to all major cities and towns within the state. For orders above $1000, we offer free delivery. Outside our regular delivery zone, additional charges may apply.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept bank transfers, credit cards, and digital payments. Terms of payment can be discussed for established customers with large orders.

How can I verify your license?

Our pharmacy license number is WH-PH-2022-08765. You can verify it with the State Pharmacy Board through their online verification system or by contacting them directly.
